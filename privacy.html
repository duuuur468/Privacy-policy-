Welcome to the privacy policy for Tic Tac Toe Classic XO. This document explains how we handle information in our free mobile game ("the App").

The App does NOT collect or store personal information. However, third-party services may collect anonymized data:

Opt-Out Options

Control ad personalization:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Tracking
